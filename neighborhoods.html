By Staff Reporter ¬∑ Feb 21, 2025 ¬∑ 4 min read

A proposed mixed-use development on the Genesee riverfront has drawn fierce opposition from longtime Corn Hill residents who say the 200-unit project will dramatically alter the historic neighborhood's character. At a packed community board meeting Tuesday, developer representatives faced two hours of pointed questions about parking, traffic, and the fate of two existing buildings that would be demolished to make way for the complex. The city planning board is expected to vote on the environmental impact study in April.
